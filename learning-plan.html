My Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to be able to make a positive difference on the world through the use of tech. I like the idea of projects like vaxx.nz and Time in the line where a technical solution can be made to improve a problem in the world.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

Strengths:

  • I am motiviated to achieve goals when things are difficult.
  • When I get stuck on something I don't give up and will try to come up with a solution
  • I am organised and when struggling with something will attempt to become more structured with my learning by generally listing out what I am stuck with and breaking it out into more managable chunks.

Weaknesses

  • I can struggle to stop thinking about a particular problem and get frustrated with minimal progress
  • I doubt my ability to achieve some tasks - sometimes challenges can feel too technical and out of my depth but then I surprise myself when I manage to figure it out
  • I find it difficult to visualise something just in my head and so I find it difficult to picture what it will look like until I see it with my eyes

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Technical skills

I would like to be able to leave Dev Academy with enough knowledge to be able come up with a solution to a problem if I identify it. I don't expect to know everything but with enough knowledge that I would be able to figure it out for myself.

Non Technical skills

I would like to learn how to have more fun/be less serious while still achieving high results.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include: An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, facilitators and industry/community representatives

For the foundations coursework I will make completion of this my priority each week. I will look ahead at the following sprints and identify what sprints I may need to dedicate more time on and where I might be familiar with the content.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

I will try to be mindful of the rising stress levels and attempt to put it into perspective before it becomes too overwhelming. If I find myself stuck I will work on identifying what it is that is making me stressed and break it down to find a way forward. I will communicate how I am going to the facilitators and reach out for support if I find I am needing it. I will also use exercise as a way to step a way from the problem and give my mind a break as to come back to the issue feeling more fresh

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

If there is something I am stuck on I will first try and resolve by myself by trying to isolate the problem and use online resources to troubleshoot what's going on. If I cannot resolve by myself I will post of the foundations channel or during bootcamp ask another student for assistance. If still stuck I will seek the guidance from a facilitator.
